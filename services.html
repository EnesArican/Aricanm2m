Made to Measure

Be it for the boardroom or for one of life’s momentous occasions, few things feel so luxurious as a personally tailored suit!

Made to measure clothing is our speciality. We can tailor a luxurious garment according to your preference with your choice of fabric and personalisation options. But most important of all, tailoring to your exact measurements means that perfection and comfort is inevitable for our clients.

We take meticulous care designing a suit to mirror the occasion. We encourage each client to be as involved as possible throughout the process, valuing any input. For a consultation on made to measure clothing, please book an appointment as soon as possible.
