Discover True Quality

Arican Made to Measure brings elite tailoring to a mass audience in the 21st century. We combine skills and expertise in traditional suit making techniques with modern cutting edge technology to provide a unique service to our customers. Our modern processes have enabled us to dispel the myth that high quality comes at a heavy price.

We have an experienced team of tailors based at our London workshop, providing advice on all styles of tailored suits, shirts and other bespoke tailored garments. If you are looking for business suits, wedding suits, a casual tailored jacket or tuxedo for a formal occasion, or even just a winter coat to keep away the cold, we can design something to make you look and feel special.
